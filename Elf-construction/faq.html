Frequently asked questions

Have in-depth discussions with the client to understand their needs and goals for the project. Develop a comprehensive project scope document outlining all deliverables, requirements, and success criteria.

Work with estimators to perform detailed cost estimation for all aspects of the project, including materials, equipment, labor, logistics, etc. Establish clear project budgets with contingencies. Develop a detailed project schedule mapping out phases, tasks, dependencies, and timelines.

Architects and engineers carry out site surveys and extensive design development to produce complete permit drawings and specifications covering architectural, structural, MEP, etc. components.

Develop detailed site-specific health and safety plans addressing training, PPE, access, egress, hazardous materials handling, fall protection, lifts, tools, etc. Assign site safety manager.

Develop change order process and documentation defining scope changes, costs, and schedule impacts. Secure approvals prior to proceeding.

Compile warranties on equipment/materials and contractor workmanship. Document as-builts. Address any punch list items prior to handover.
